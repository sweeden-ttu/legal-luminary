## Legal Aid & Assistance

### Low-Income Legal Services

**Legal Aid of Central Texas** — Provides free civil legal services to qualifying low-income residents. Services include family law, housing, consumer issues, and public benefits.

**Texas RioGrande Legal Aid** — Additional legal assistance for qualifying individuals.

**Lone Star Legal Aid** — Free civil legal services in certain Texas regions.

### Self-Help Resources

**TexasLawHelp.org** — Free legal information and forms for Texans who cannot afford an attorney.

**Texas Access to Justice Foundation** — Resources for navigating the legal system.

## Victim Services

**Bell County Crime Victim Assistance** — (254) 933-5215 — Support services for crime victims

**Families in Crisis (Killeen)** — (254) 634-1184 — Domestic violence services

**Hope Alliance (Williamson County)** — Services for domestic violence and sexual assault survivors in the broader Central Texas area

**Bell County Jail** — (254) 933-5400 — 111 W. Central Avenue, Belton, TX 76513
